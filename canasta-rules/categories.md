# Canasta Rules Categories

## Core Gameplay

### `setup`
Game setup, number of decks, dealing, initial meld requirements based on score.

### `drawing`
Rules for drawing from the stock pile vs taking the discard pile.

### `melding`
Creating new melds, adding to existing melds, meld requirements.

### `wild_cards`
Using Jokers and 2s in melds, limits on wild cards per meld.

### `freezing`
When and how the discard pile becomes frozen, unfreezing rules.

### `canastas`
- Clean (natural) canastas — no wild cards
- Dirty (mixed) canastas — with wild cards
- Requirements, bonuses, limits

### `going_out`
Requirements to go out, asking partner, concealed going out.

### `scoring`
Point values for cards, bonuses, penalties, round scoring.

## Special Rules

### `special_hands`
Any special hand combinations and their rules.

### `special_cards`
- Red 3s — bonuses and requirements
- Black 3s — blocking the pile, discard rules
- Other special card rules

### `partners`
Partnership communication, signaling (if any), team play.

## Table Play

### `etiquette`
Common courtesies, turn order, handling disputes.
