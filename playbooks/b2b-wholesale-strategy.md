*Created: March 12, 2026*
*Status: Planning — execution blocked until smaller carton sizes arrive (ETA: May-June 2026)*

---

## Current Blockers

- **Carton size:** Current 24-unit master cartons are too large for indie retailers. New PO will include 6 or 12-unit cartons.
- **No B2B landing pages:** Need dedicated wholesale pages on all7s.co
- **No B2B marketing plan yet**
- **CPC compliance:** CANSET is compliant. Rolling Jokers needs marble fix before B2B expansion.

## Strategic Overview

**Primary B2B channel:** Faire (best deal for retailers — net 60, free returns, discovery)
**Direct wholesale:** Shopify B2B (for larger accounts, custom pricing)
**Retail targets:** Barnes & Noble, Books-A-Million (2027 timeline)
**Outreach list:** Playmobil retailer email list (leaked, ~hundreds of indie toy/game stores)

**Why Faire over direct Shopify B2B for most retailers:**
- Retailers prefer Faire: net 60 terms, Faire-backed returns, discovery marketplace
- Lower friction for first-time buyers
- Faire handles payment risk
- Drive B2B traffic to Faire; reserve Shopify B2B for large/recurring accounts
