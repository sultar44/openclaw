# Fridge Moments — Launch Checklist

**Business:** Custom photo fridge magnets
**Owner:** Gianna Gonzalez (age 10), with Ramon's support
**Domain:** fridgemoments.com
**Budget:** $1,000 total
**Email:** hello@fridgemoments.com

---

## Phase 1 — Validate ($50-80 equipment + ~$50 tech)

### Equipment (Week 1)
- [ ] Pre-cut inkjet magnet sheets (~$15-20 for 25 sheets, Amazon)
- [ ] Photo paper if needed for home printer
- [ ] Paper cutter or craft knife + cutting mat
- [ ] Small poly mailers or rigid mailers (~$10 for 25)
- [ ] Thank-you card stock (she handwrites these — brand differentiator)
- [ ] Packing tape, labels

### Tech Setup (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Shopify Basic ($39/mo, first month usually $1 trial)
- [ ] Install photo upload app (Uploadery, Product Customizer, or Kickflip — compare free trials)
- [ ] Set up fridgemoments.com on Shopify
- [ ] Route hello@fridgemoments.com to Ramon's email
- [ ] Shopify Email enabled (free up to 10K emails/mo)
- [ ] Shopify Payments activated (Ramon as account holder)
- [ ] Set up 1-2 products: single magnet, 3-pack, 5-pack (volume discount)
- [ ] Basic shipping rates (USPS First Class, flat rate)

### Branding (Week 1)
- [ ] Gianna designs logo in Canva (let her own this!)
- [ ] Simple color palette (2-3 colors)
- [ ] "Made by Gianna" messaging throughout
- [ ] Product photos — she makes sample magnets for family photos, photographs them on a fridge

### Profit Tracker (Week 1)
- [ ] Google Sheet with columns: Date, Order #, Revenue, Material Cost, Fees, Shipping Cost, Profit
- [ ] Running totals and monthly summary
- [ ] Color coded: green = profit, red = loss
- [ ] Gianna enters data after each order (learning exercise)

### Seed Sales (Week 2)
- [ ] 3-5 orders from family/friends (real orders, real payment)
- [ ] Gianna fulfills the full cycle: receive order → download photo → print → cut → package → ship
- [ ] This validates the workflow AND gives her the dopamine hit before going public

## Content Strategy (Starting Week 2-3)

### Platform Priority
1. **TikTok** (Ramon's account, featuring Gianna) — kid entrepreneur content explodes here
2. **Instagram Reels** — same content, cross-post
3. **YouTube Shorts** — same content, cross-post

### Content Pillars
- **Making videos:** Gianna making magnets from start to finish (satisfying process content)
- **Reveal videos:** Showing the finished magnet vs the original photo
- **Story videos:** "A grandma ordered this for her fridge..." (emotional hook)
- **Behind the scenes:** Packaging orders, checking inventory, "CEO stuff"
- **Milestone videos:** First sale, 10th sale, first bad review, etc.

### Content Tips
- Film vertically, good lighting, close-up on hands during process
- Let Gianna's personality shine — don't over-script
- Hook in first 2 seconds: "Watch me turn this photo into a fridge magnet"
- Post 3-5x per week minimum during launch phase

## Phase 2 — Upgrade (After 25 sales milestone)

### Equipment Upgrade ($400-500)
- [ ] Sublimation printer (Epson EcoTank ET-2800 ~$200 or Sawgrass SG500 ~$400)
- [ ] Sublimation ink set
- [ ] Sublimation-ready magnet blanks (various sizes)
- [ ] Small heat press (~$100-150)
- [ ] This drops per-unit cost significantly and improves quality

### Add Etsy ($0 upfront)
- [ ] Create Etsy shop (Ramon as account holder)
- [ ] Mirror Shopify products
- [ ] Etsy SEO: target "custom photo magnet", "personalized fridge magnet", "photo gift"
- [ ] Etsy has built-in search traffic — people looking for exactly this

### Expand Product Line
- [ ] Different sizes (mini, standard, large)
- [ ] Multi-packs (family set, pet set)
- [ ] Seasonal themes (holiday magnets, back-to-school)
- [ ] Custom shapes (heart, circle, star)

## Pricing Suggestion

### Phase 1 (Inkjet magnets)
- Single magnet: $8-10
- 3-pack: $22-25
- 5-pack: $35-40
- Material cost per magnet: ~$1.50-2.00
- Shopify fees: ~3% + $0.30
- Shipping: $3-4 (USPS First Class)
- **Profit per single magnet: ~$4-5**

### Phase 2 (Sublimation magnets)
- Premium quality allows slight price increase
- Material cost drops to ~$0.75-1.00 per magnet
- **Profit per single magnet: ~$5-7**
