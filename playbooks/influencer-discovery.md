## Weekly Process (Mondays 1 AM EST)

### 1. Check existing sheet
- Read current Influencers and Brands tabs to avoid duplicates

### 2. Discovery
- Search IG, TikTok, FB for new mahjong/canasta creators
- Search terms: "canasta", "mahjong", "game night", "card games for women", "mahjong teacher"
- Look at followers/following of existing influencers for new leads
- Check hashtags: #mahjong, #canasta, #gamenightideas, #mahjongteacher

### 3. Classification
For each new account found:
- Visit their profile via browser
- Determine: Influencer (teacher/lifestyle/community) or Brand?
- Collect: handle, platform, name, follower count, niche, content type
- Look for contact info (email in bio, DM preference)
- Add to appropriate tab

### 4. Quality filters
- Minimum 500 followers (skip tiny/inactive accounts)
- Must have posted within last 3 months
- Content should be relevant (not just one random post about mahjong)

### 5. Reporting
- Post summary to #chloebot: "Found X new influencers, Y new brands this week"
- Only alert if high-value find (>10K followers, active canasta content)
